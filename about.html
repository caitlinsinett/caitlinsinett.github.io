About Me, Caitlin Sinett

I am a journalism major at the University of Florida in Gainesville, Fla., who is interested in print and broadcast journalism. I especially enjoy reporting on features, politics and health. Reporting is my passion, and my goal is to become a reporter for a news outlet either in broadcast or print. I have previous experience in both. I also have experience in producing online news videos and reading and producing for radio. For more information, you can check out my resume and portfolio.

This summer I will be interning in Washington, D.C., with The Washington Center program, and I am excited to explore the area where the journalism world is centered. I hope to gain a lot of experience from the program and get comfortable and familiarized with the D.C. area.
